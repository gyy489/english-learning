# A Quiet Morning in the Garden

- 天数：第 18 天
- 来源真题：无（纯生词复习）
- 来源文件：无（纯生词复习）
- 复习内容：D17 生词复用 + 纯生词复习

## 正文

1. I woke up early and stayed awake in my quiet room.

   > 我很早醒来，在安静的房间里保持清醒。
   >

2. I did not expect the morning to be so warm.

   > 我没有想到早晨会这么暖和。
   >

3. My mother called me warmly from the kitchen.

   > 妈妈从厨房热情地叫我。
   >

4. She asked me to stir the soup with a spoon.

   > 她让我用勺子搅拌汤。
   >

5. The soup was not too dry, but it needed a little more water.

   > 汤并不太干，但需要再加一点水。
   >

6. After breakfast, I went into the garden behind our house.

   > 早饭后，我走进了屋后的花园。
   >

7. The path in the garden was uneven after the rain.

   > 雨后花园里的小路变得不平坦。
   >

8. I walked slowly because I did not want to fall.

   > 我慢慢走，因为我不想摔倒。
   >

9. A small round stone was near the door.

   > 门附近有一块小圆石头。
   >

10. I moved it to the side with my foot.

   > 我用脚把它移到一边。
   >

11. Then I tried to reach a red apple on a low branch.

   > 然后我试着够到低树枝上的一个红苹果。
   >

12. The apple had a funny shape, but it looked fresh.

   > 这个苹果形状有点奇怪，但看起来很新鲜。
   >

13. My father came out with a blue tie in his hand.

   > 爸爸手里拿着一条蓝色领带走了出来。
   >

14. He asked me to help him tie it.

   > 他让我帮他系上它。
   >

15. I laughed because he was going to work in a modern office.

   > 我笑了，因为他要去一个现代办公室上班。
   >

16. He said old hands can still learn modern things.

   > 他说老手也能学习现代事物。
   >

17. Near the fence, our dog was chewing a small bone.

   > 篱笆旁边，我们的狗正在啃一小块骨头。
   >

18. My mother told us that some food can be harmful to dogs.

   > 妈妈告诉我们，有些食物可能对狗有害。
   >

19. So we checked the bone and took away the small sharp pieces.

   > 所以我们检查了骨头，并拿走了小而尖的部分。
   >

20. My sister wanted to trade her apple for my orange.

   > 妹妹想用她的苹果换我的橙子。
   >

21. I agreed because I liked apples more that day.

   > 我同意了，因为那天我更喜欢苹果。
   >

22. Later, we began to argue about who should water the plants.

   > 后来，我们开始争论谁应该给植物浇水。
   >

23. The argument was small, and we soon smiled again.

   > 这场争论很小，我们很快又笑了。
   >

24. We used dry leaves to cover the soil around the flowers.

   > 我们用干叶子盖住花周围的土。
   >

25. The garden looked simple, clean, and peaceful.

   > 花园看起来简单、干净又安静。
   >

26. I did not expect such a normal morning to feel so nice.

   > 我没想到这样一个普通的早晨会让人感觉这么好。
   >
