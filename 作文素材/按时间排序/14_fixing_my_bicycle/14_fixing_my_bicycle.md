# Fixing My Bicycle on a Rainy Afternoon

- 天数：第 14 天
- 时间线索：Last Saturday afternoon
- 复习内容：D13 生词复用

## 复习生词

- nervous - 紧张的；焦虑的
- restriction - 限制；约束
- uncertainty - 不确定；不确定性
- sound - 听起来；发出声音
- forward - 向前；前进
- pronunciation - 发音
- discovery - 发现
- bring - 带来；拿来
- unpredictably - 不可预测地
- weak - 虚弱的；薄弱的
- whole - 整个的；全部的
- improvement - 改善；进步

## 正文

1. Last Saturday afternoon, I rode my bicycle to a small repair shop near the station.

   > 上周六下午，我骑自行车去了车站附近的一家小修理店。
   >
2. The sky was dark, and rain started falling unpredictably while I was on the road.

   > 天空很暗，我在路上时，雨开始不可预测地（unpredictably）下了起来。
   >
3. At first, I felt nervous because the back wheel sounded strange.

   > 一开始，我感到紧张（nervous），因为后轮听起来（sounded）很奇怪。
   >
4. Each time I pushed the bicycle forward, the sound became louder.

   > 每当我把自行车向前（forward）推时，那个声音就变得更大。
   >
5. I was afraid that the whole wheel might suddenly stop moving.

   > 我担心整个（whole）车轮可能会突然不转了。
   >
6. When I arrived at the shop, the mechanic looked at the bicycle carefully.

   > 当我到达修理店时，修车师傅仔细看了看自行车。
   >
7. He said the problem was not serious, but there was one small restriction.

   > 他说问题并不严重，但有一个小限制（restriction）。
   >
8. I could not ride too fast for the next two days.

   > 接下来的两天里，我不能骑得太快。
   >
9. Because of the rain, there was still some uncertainty about when the repair would be finished.

   > 因为下雨，维修什么时候完成仍然有一些不确定性（uncertainty）。
   >
10. I waited inside the shop and listened to the rain hitting the window.

    > 我在店里等待，听着雨打在窗户上的声音。
    >
11. The mechanic asked me where I lived, but I was not sure about the pronunciation of my street name.

    > 修车师傅问我住在哪里，但我不太确定我那条街名字的发音（pronunciation）。
    >
12. I showed him the address on my phone, and he immediately understood.

    > 我把手机上的地址给他看，他马上就明白了。
    >
13. While he worked, I made a small discovery about my bicycle.

    > 他修车的时候，我对自己的自行车有了一个小发现（discovery）。
    >
14. The chain was weak because I had not cleaned it for a long time.

    > 车链很脆弱（weak），因为我很久没有清洁它了。
    >
15. This simple discovery brought me a useful lesson.

    > 这个简单的发现带给（brought）我一个有用的教训。
    >
16. Small problems become bigger when I ignore them for too long.

    > 当我忽视小问题太久时，它们就会变成更大的问题。
    >
17. After about twenty minutes, the strange sound disappeared.

    > 大约二十分钟后，那个奇怪的声音消失了。
    >
18. The bicycle felt smoother, and I could clearly notice the improvement.

    > 自行车感觉更顺了，我可以清楚地注意到这种改善（improvement）。
    >
19. On my way home, I rode slowly through the wet streets.

    > 回家的路上，我慢慢骑过潮湿的街道。
    >
20. I realized that taking care of small things early can make daily life easier.

    > 我意识到，早点处理小事情可以让日常生活更轻松。
    >
