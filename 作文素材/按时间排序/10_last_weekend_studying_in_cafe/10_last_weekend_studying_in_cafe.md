# Studying in a Small Cafe

- 天数：第 10 天
- 时间线索：Last weekend
- 复习内容：常见生单词 / D6-复习

Last weekend, I decided to study in a small café near my apartment.  
> 上周末，我决定去公寓附近的一家小咖啡馆学习。  
I usually work at home, but after spending many days in the same room, I started to feel bored and unfocused.  
> 我通常在家工作，但在同一个房间待了多日后，开始感到厌倦且注意力不集中 (unfocused)。  
I thought that changing my environment might help my concentration.  
> 我认为改变环境 (environment) 可能会有助于提高我的专注力 (concentration)。  
When I arrived at the café, I noticed several people quietly reading or working on their computers.  
> 到达咖啡馆时，我注意到有几个人在安静地阅读或使用电脑工作。  
The atmosphere was calm, and soft music was playing in the background.  
> 氛围 (atmosphere) 很平静，背景播放着柔和的音乐。  
Although the place was not completely silent, it still felt comfortable and peaceful.  
> 尽管那里并非完全寂静，但依然让人感到舒适、宁静 (peaceful)。  
I ordered a coffee, opened my notebook, and began practicing my English pronunciation by reading short texts aloud very quietly.  
> 我点了一杯咖啡，打开笔记本，通过极轻声地朗读短文来练习英语发音 (pronunciation)。  
At first, studying there felt a little strange because I was not used to working around other people.  
> 起初，在那里学习感觉有些奇怪，因为我不习惯在他人身边工作。  
However, after about twenty minutes, I became more relaxed and focused.  
> 然而，大约 20 分钟后，我变得更加放松且专注 (focused)。  
I also observed that I checked my phone less often than usual.  
> 我还观察到 (observed) 自己看手机的次数比平时少了。  
Since there were fewer distractions, I could continue studying for longer periods without losing attention.  
> 由于干扰 (distractions) 减少，我可以持续学习更长时间而不分心。  
Before leaving, I realized something important.  
> 离开前，我意识到 (realized) 了一些重要的事。  
Sometimes we do not need extra motivation or complicated methods.  
> 有时我们不需要额外的动力 (motivation) 或复杂的方法。  
Small changes in our daily routine can improve the way we think and work more efficiently.  
> 日常习惯 (routine) 中的微小改变就能优化思维方式，让我们工作得更有效率 (efficiently)。
