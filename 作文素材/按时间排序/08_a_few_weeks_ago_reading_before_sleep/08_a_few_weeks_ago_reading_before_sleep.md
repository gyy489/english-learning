# Reading Before Sleep

- 时间线索：A few weeks ago
- 来源：主题1 / 第 5 篇

1. For a long time, I had a habit of using my phone late at night.  
    > 很长一段时间里，我都有晚上很晚玩手机的习惯（habit）。  
1. I often watched short videos or read random information on the internet before going to sleep.  
    > 我经常在睡觉前刷短视频，或者浏览网上各种随机（random）的信息。  
1. Although this helped me relax for a short time, I usually slept very late and felt tired the next morning.  
    > 虽然这能让我短暂放松（relax），但我通常会睡得很晚，并且第二天早上感到疲惫（tired）。  
1. Sometimes I even woke up during the night and found it difficult to fall asleep again.  
    > 有时候，我甚至会半夜醒来，并发现很难再次入睡（fall asleep）。  

⸻

1. A few weeks ago, I decided to change this habit.  
    > 几周前，我决定改变这个习惯。  
1. Instead of looking at a screen before bed, I started reading a physical book for twenty or thirty minutes every night.  
    > 我不再睡前看屏幕，而是开始每天晚上读二三十分钟纸质书（physical book）。  
1. At first, it was not easy because my attention moved quickly from one thing to another.  
    > 一开始并不容易，因为我的注意力（attention）总是很快从一件事转移到另一件事。  
1. I also felt bored sometimes because reading was slower than watching videos online.  
    > 我有时也会觉得无聊（bored），因为阅读比在线看视频更慢。  

⸻

1. However, after continuing this routine for some time, I began to notice several changes.  
    > 然而，在坚持（continue）这个日常习惯（routine）一段时间后，我开始注意到一些变化。  
1. My sleep became more regular, and I started feeling calmer before bedtime.  
    > 我的睡眠变得更加规律（regular），而且我在睡前开始感觉更加平静（calm）。  
1. Reading also improved my concentration during the day.  
    > 阅读还提高了我白天的专注力（concentration）。  
1. I found that I could focus on my work for longer periods without checking my phone every few minutes.  
    > 我发现，自己能够更长时间专注（focus on）工作，而不会每隔几分钟就查看手机。  

⸻

1. Now, reading at night has become one of the most peaceful parts of my daily routine.  
    > 现在，夜晚阅读已经成为我日常生活（daily routine）中最平静（peaceful）的部分之一。  
1. It helps me slow down, clear my mind, and end the day more quietly.  
    > 它帮助我慢下来、清理（clear）思绪，并让一天在更加安静的状态中结束。
