# Riding to the Library After the Repair

- 天数：第 15 天
- 时间线索：The next morning
- 复习内容：D14 生词复用

## 复习生词

- ride - 骑；乘坐
- wheel - 轮子；车轮
- hit - 打；撞击；碰到
- wet - 湿的；潮湿的
- through - 穿过；通过
- smooth - 平滑的；顺利的
- ignore - 忽视；不理会
- suddenly - 突然地
- bring - 带来；拿来
- chain - 链条；链子
- mechanic - 机械师；修理工
- repair - 修理；维修
- road - 路；道路

## 正文

1. The next morning, I decided to ride my bicycle to the library.

   > 第二天早上，我决定骑（ride）自行车去图书馆。
   >
2. I wanted to test the bicycle after the repair.

   > 我想在维修（repair）之后测试一下自行车。
   >
3. Before I left home, I checked the back wheel carefully.

   > 离开家之前，我仔细检查了后轮（wheel）。
   >
4. The wheel looked clean, and it did not make any strange sound.

   > 车轮看起来很干净，而且没有发出任何奇怪的声音。
   >
5. I also touched the chain to see if it was too loose.

   > 我还摸了摸链条（chain），看看它是不是太松。
   >
6. The mechanic had cleaned it well, so it moved smoothly.

   > 修车师傅（mechanic）已经把它清洁好了，所以它转动得很顺滑（smoothly）。
   >
7. The road outside my building was still wet from last night's rain.

   > 我家楼外面的道路（road）仍然因为昨晚的雨而潮湿（wet）。
   >
8. I rode slowly because I did not want the bicycle to slide.

   > 我骑得很慢，因为我不想让自行车打滑。
   >
9. When I went through a small street, a car suddenly stopped in front of me.

   > 当我穿过（through）一条小街时，一辆车突然（suddenly）停在我前面。
   >
10. I held the brakes quickly, and my heart beat faster for a few seconds.

    > 我很快捏住刹车，心跳快了几秒钟。
    >
11. Nothing hit my bicycle, but the moment still made me nervous.

    > 没有什么撞到（hit）我的自行车，但那一刻还是让我紧张。
    >
12. I waited for the car to move away before I continued.

    > 我等那辆车开走后才继续前进。
    >
13. This time, I did not ignore the small warning in my mind.

    > 这一次，我没有忽视（ignore）脑子里的小提醒。
    >
14. I slowed down and paid more attention to the people around me.

    > 我放慢速度，更加注意周围的人。
    >
15. After ten minutes, the ride became easier and more comfortable.

    > 十分钟后，这次骑行变得更轻松、更舒服。
    >
16. The clean chain brought a quiet feeling to the whole bicycle.

    > 干净的链条给（brought）整辆自行车带来了一种安静的感觉。
    >
17. I could hear only the soft sound of the wheels on the wet road.

    > 我只能听到车轮在潮湿道路上的轻柔声音。
    >
18. When I arrived at the library, I parked the bicycle near the entrance.

    > 到达图书馆时，我把自行车停在入口附近。
    >
19. I felt glad because the repair really made my ride smoother.

    > 我感到高兴，因为这次维修确实让我的骑行更顺畅（smoother）。
    >
20. I learned that taking care of a small problem can bring a safer day.

    > 我学到，处理好一个小问题可以带来更安全的一天。
    >
