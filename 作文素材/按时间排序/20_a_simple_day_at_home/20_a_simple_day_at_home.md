# A Simple Day at Home

- 天数：第 20 天
- 来源真题：无（纯生词复习）
- 来源文件：无（纯生词复习）
- 复习内容：D19 当前生词 + 到期旧词 + 积压清理（纯单词）

- dry - 干的；弄干
- branch - 树枝；分支
- reach - 到达；伸手够到

- chew - 咀嚼

## 正文

1. This morning, I woke up early and went to the kitchen.

   > 今天早上，我很早醒来，然后去了厨房。
   >

2. I made a cup of tea and used a spoon to stir it.

   > 我泡了一杯茶，并用勺子搅拌它。
   >

3. The bread on the table was too dry.

   > 桌子上的面包太干了。
   >

4. I put a little milk on it so it was easier to chew.

   > 我在上面放了一点牛奶，所以它更容易咀嚼。
   >

5. My mother came in and said my face looked weak.

   > 我妈妈走进来说我的脸色看起来很虚弱。
   >

6. I told her I just needed a slow morning.

   > 我告诉她我只是需要一个慢一点的早晨。
   >

7. After breakfast, I went outside to get some fresh air.

   > 早餐后，我出去呼吸一些新鲜空气。
   >

8. A small bird was sitting on a branch near the window.

   > 一只小鸟正停在窗户旁边的一根树枝上。
   >

9. I tried to reach my phone to take a photo.

   > 我试着伸手够我的手机去拍一张照片。
   >

10. The phone was on a high shelf, so I had to stand on a chair.

   > 手机在一个高架子上，所以我不得不站在椅子上。
   >

11. I was aware that this was not very safe.

   > 我意识到这不是很安全。
   >

12. I got down and asked my brother for help.

   > 我下来并请我哥哥帮忙。
   >

13. He gave me the phone and told me to be careful.

   > 他把手机给了我，并告诉我要小心。
   >

14. The photo was not clear, but I still liked it.

   > 照片不清楚，但我仍然喜欢它。
   >

15. Later, I sat at my desk and opened my notebook.

   > 后来，我坐在书桌前，打开了我的笔记本。
   >

16. I wanted to record some small things about the morning.

   > 我想记录一些关于早晨的小事。
   >

17. I wrote one short paragraph about the bird.

   > 我写了一个关于那只鸟的短段落。
   >

18. I tried to describe its brown body and quick eyes.

   > 我试着描述它棕色的身体和灵活的眼睛。
   >

19. Then I made a contrast between the quiet bird and the busy street.

   > 然后我把安静的小鸟和忙碌的街道作了对比。
   >

20. The street was loud, but the bird stayed calm.

   > 街道很吵，但小鸟保持安静。
   >

21. At noon, my brother asked me to trade my apple for his banana.

   > 中午，我哥哥让我用我的苹果换他的香蕉。
   >

22. I said yes because I wanted something soft.

   > 我同意了，因为我想要一些软的东西。
   >

23. He used a sharp knife to cut the apple.

   > 他用一把锋利的刀切苹果。
   >

24. I told him to keep the knife away from the table edge.

   > 我告诉他把刀放远一点，不要靠近桌边。
   >

25. In the afternoon, I helped my father tie a box with string.

   > 下午，我帮爸爸用绳子绑一个盒子。
   >

26. There was a restriction on the box, so we could not put heavy things inside.

   > 盒子有一个限制，所以我们不能把重东西放进去。
   >

27. At night, I read my notes again and felt better about my day.

   > 晚上，我又读了一遍我的笔记，并对这一天感觉更好了。
   >
