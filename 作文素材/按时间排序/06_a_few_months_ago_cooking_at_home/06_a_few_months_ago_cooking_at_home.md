# Cooking More Often at Home

- 时间线索：A few months ago
- 来源：主题1 / 第 4 篇

A few months ago, I started cooking more often at home.  
> 几个月前，我开始更频繁地在家做饭。  

Before that, I usually bought prepared food or ordered meals online because it was faster and more convenient.  
> 在那之前，我通常买现成的食物或者点外卖，因为那样更快、更方便。  

After long days of studying or working, I did not want to spend extra time in the kitchen.  
> 在经历了一整天的学习或工作之后，我不想再花额外的时间待在厨房里。  

However, over time, I noticed that this habit was becoming expensive and unhealthy.  
> 然而，随着时间推移，我注意到这种习惯变得既昂贵又不健康。  

At first, cooking felt difficult to me.  
> 一开始，做饭对我来说很困难。  

I did not know how to prepare many simple dishes, and I often worried about making mistakes.  
> 我不知道如何制作许多简单的菜肴，而且我经常担心出错。  

Sometimes the food was too salty, sometimes it was overcooked, and sometimes I forgot important ingredients.  
> 有时候食物太咸，有时候煮过头了，还有时候我会忘记重要的食材。  

Even so, I continued practicing because I wanted to become more independent.  
> 即便如此，我还是继续练习，因为我想变得更加独立。  

Little by little, I became more comfortable in the kitchen.  
> 渐渐地，我在厨房里变得更加从容。  

I learned how to organize my time better and prepare meals more efficiently.  
> 我学会了如何更好地安排时间，并更高效地准备食物。  

Cooking also changed the way I think about food.  
> 做饭也改变了我看待食物的方式。  

Instead of eating quickly without paying attention, I started to enjoy the process of preparing and sharing meals.  
> 我不再只是匆忙地吃饭而不去注意食物，而是开始享受准备和分享食物的过程。  

Now, cooking is no longer something stressful for me.  
> 现在，做饭对我来说已经不再是一件有压力的事情。  

It has become a quiet daily activity that helps me slow down and take better care of myself.  
> 它已经变成了一种安静的日常活动，帮助我放慢节奏，并更好地照顾自己。
