# A Simple Way to Learn English

- 时间线索：When I started learning English (no exact date)
- 来源：主题1 / 第 2 篇

1. When I started learning English, I wanted to improve very quickly.  
    > 当我开始学习英语时，我想非常快地提高（improve）。  
1. I watched many videos about language learning, downloaded several applications, and bought different grammar books.  
    > 我看了很多关于语言学习（language learning）的视频，下载了几个应用程序（applications），还买了不同的语法书（grammar books）。  
1. However, after a few weeks, I realized that I was spending more time preparing to learn than actually learning.  
    > 然而，几周后，我意识到（realize）自己花在“准备学习”上的时间，比真正学习的时间还多。  

⸻

1. One evening, I decided to change my method.  
    > 一天晚上，我决定改变我的学习方法（method）。  
1. Instead of studying many different things at the same time, I began to focus on only one short English text every day.  
    > 我不再同时学习很多不同的内容，而是开始每天只专注（focus on）一篇简短的英语文章。  
1. First, I listened to the audio several times without reading.  
    > 首先，我在不看文本的情况下听了好几遍音频（audio）。  
1. Then I read the text carefully and checked the words that I did not understand.  
    > 然后我认真阅读文章，并查阅（check）那些我不懂的单词。  
1. After that, I repeated the sentences aloud and tried to copy the pronunciation and rhythm.  
    > 之后，我大声重复这些句子，并努力模仿（copy）它们的发音（pronunciation）和节奏（rhythm）。  

⸻

1. At the beginning, this process felt slow and sometimes even boring.  
    > 一开始，这个过程（process）感觉很慢，有时甚至有些无聊（boring）。  
1. I could not remember many sentences, and I often forgot words after only one day.  
    > 我记不住很多句子，而且经常只过一天就忘记单词。  
1. But little by little, I noticed a change.  
    > 但渐渐地（little by little），我注意到了一些变化（change）。  
1. Some expressions started to feel natural, and I became more comfortable when listening to simple English conversations.  
    > 一些表达（expressions）开始变得自然（natural）起来，而且我在听简单英语对话（conversations）时也变得更加自在（comfortable）。  

⸻

1. Now, I understand that language learning is not a race.  
    > 现在，我明白了语言学习并不是一场竞赛（race）。  
1. Progress usually happens quietly, through small daily practice repeated many times.  
    > 进步（progress）通常是在安静中发生的，它来自许多次重复的小规模日常练习（daily practice）。
