# Reviewing One Paragraph in the Evening

- 时间线索：Yesterday evening

## 正文

* Yesterday evening, I chose one short paragraph from an old English article.

  > 昨天晚上，我从一篇旧英语文章中选了一小段（paragraph）。
  >
* I wanted to read it slowly and understand every sentence clearly.

  > 我想慢慢读它，并清楚地理解每一个句子。
  >
* Before I began, I gave myself a simple restriction.

  > 在开始之前，我给自己设定了一个简单的限制（restriction）。
  >
* I could not look up every word immediately during the first reading.

  > 第一次阅读期间（during），我不能马上查每一个单词。
  >
* This rule made me feel a little nervous because there was still some uncertainty.

  > 这个规则让我有点紧张，因为仍然有一些不确定性（uncertainty）。
  >
* However, it also helped me guess the meaning through the whole sentence.

  > 然而，它也帮助我通过（through）整个句子来猜意思。
  >
* At first, my voice sounded weak when I read the paragraph aloud.

  > 一开始，当我大声读这段文字时，我的声音听起来（sounded）很弱。
  >
* I was too focused on each word, so the sentences did not sound natural.

  > 我太专注于每一个单词，所以句子听起来不自然。
  >
* After the second reading, I became more aware of the rhythm of the sentences.

  > 第二次阅读后，我更加意识到（aware）句子的节奏。
  >
* I noticed a clear contrast between slow reading and nervous reading.

  > 我注意到慢速朗读和紧张朗读之间有明显的反差（contrast）。
  >
* Slow reading made my pronunciation clearer, but nervous reading made me forget easy words.

  > 慢速朗读让我的发音更清楚，但紧张朗读让我忘记简单的单词。
  >
* This small discovery brought me a useful idea.

  > 这个小发现带给（brought）我一个有用的想法。
  >
* I did not need to read faster to sound better.

  > 我不需要读得更快，才能听起来更好。
  >
* I needed to read with more attention and more patience.

  > 我需要带着更多注意力和更多耐心去读。
  >
* After reading, I described my problem in my notebook.

  > 读完后，我在笔记本里描述了（described）自己的问题。
  >
* I wrote, “My ending sounds are not clear enough.”

  > 我写道：“我的结尾音还不够清楚。”
  >
* I also wrote, “Pause after long sentences.”

  > 我还写道：“长句之后要停顿。”
  >
* Sometimes improvement appears unpredictably, but careful review makes it easier to notice.

  > 有时候进步会不可预测地（unpredictably）出现，但认真复习会让它更容易被发现。
  >
* By the end of the practice, the same paragraph felt much more familiar.

  > 练习结束时，同一段文字让我感觉熟悉多了。
  >
* I realized that one quiet evening with one paragraph could still help me move forward.

  > 我意识到，一个安静的夜晚加上一小段文字，仍然可以帮助我继续前进。
  >
