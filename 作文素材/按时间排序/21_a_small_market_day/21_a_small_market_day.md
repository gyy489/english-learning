# A Small Market Day

- 天数：第 21 天
- 来源真题：无（纯生词复习）
- 来源文件：无（纯生词复习）

## 正文

1. On Saturday morning, Lina went to the small market near her home.

   > 星期六早上，Lina去了她家附近的小市场。
   >

2. The air was cool, but the road was still dry.

   > 空气很凉，但路面仍然是干的。
   >

3. She carried a cloth bag and walked slowly.

   > 她拿着一个布袋，慢慢地走着。
   >

4. Lina felt a little nervous because she had to buy things alone.

   > Lina有点紧张，因为她必须一个人买东西。
   >

5. Her mother told her to stay calm and smile.

   > 她妈妈告诉她要保持冷静并微笑。
   >

6. When Lina reached the market, many people were already there.

   > 当Lina到达市场时，已经有很多人在那里了。
   >

7. She stopped to tie her shoe before she went inside.

   > 她进去之前停下来系鞋带。
   >

8. A boy near the gate was chewing a piece of bread.

   > 门口附近的一个男孩正在嚼一片面包。
   >

9. Lina saw a sharp knife on a fruit table and stepped back.

   > Lina在水果摊上看到一把锋利的刀，就往后退了一步。
   >

10. The seller smiled and moved the knife away.

   > 卖家笑了笑，把刀移开了。
   >

11. Lina wanted to trade two small apples for one big orange.

   > Lina想用两个小苹果换一个大橙子。
   >

12. The seller said yes, and Lina was happy.

   > 卖家同意了，Lina很高兴。
   >

13. She was aware that she needed to count her money carefully.

   > 她意识到自己需要仔细数钱。
   >

14. There was a small restriction at the market: each person could buy only one bag of rice.

   > 市场有一个小限制：每个人只能买一袋米。
   >

15. Lina did not need rice, so this was not a problem.

   > Lina不需要米，所以这不是问题。
   >

16. Then the weather changed unpredictably, and dark clouds came.

   > 然后天气不可预测地变了，乌云来了。
   >

17. This brought some uncertainty to her simple plan.

   > 这给她简单的计划带来了一些不确定。
   >

18. Soon she heard the sound of rain on the market roof.

   > 很快她听到了雨打在市场屋顶上的声音。
   >

19. Her best discovery was a small dry place behind a tea shop.

   > 她最好的发现是一家茶店后面的一个干燥小地方。
   >

20. Lina stood there and checked her bag.

   > Lina站在那里检查她的袋子。
   >

21. The apples were safe, and the orange was not broken.

   > 苹果没有问题，橙子也没有破。
   >

22. After ten minutes, she moved forward again.

   > 十分钟后，她又向前走。
   >

23. She bought eggs, milk, and a loaf of bread.

   > 她买了鸡蛋、牛奶和一条面包。
   >

24. She spoke more clearly to each seller.

   > 她和每个卖家说话都更清楚了。
   >

25. Lina felt a real improvement in her confidence.

   > Lina感到自己的信心有了真正的改善。
   >

26. When she got home, her mother said the shopping was well done.

   > 当她回到家时，她妈妈说这次购物做得很好。
   >

27. Lina smiled and said the market was not so hard after all.

   > Lina笑着说，市场其实没有那么难。
   >
