# Walking Without My Phone

- 时间线索：Last month
- 来源：主题1 / 第 3 篇

1. Last month, I started a new habit.  
    > 上个月，我开始了一个新的习惯（habit）。  
1. Every evening after dinner, I go for a short walk around my neighborhood without taking my phone with me.  
    > 每天晚饭后，我都会在附近社区（neighborhood）里短暂散步，而且不带手机。  
1. At first, this felt strange because I was used to listening to music, checking messages, or watching videos while walking.  
    > 一开始，这让我觉得很奇怪，因为我已经习惯（be used to）边走路边听音乐、看消息或者刷视频了。  
1. The silence made me uncomfortable for the first few days.  
    > 最初几天，这种安静（silence）让我感到不自在（uncomfortable）。  

⸻

1. However, after some time, I began to notice things that I had ignored before.  
    > 然而，过了一段时间后，我开始注意到以前忽视（ignore）的事情。  
1. I paid attention to the sound of the wind, the colors of the sky, and the lights in people’s windows.  
    > 我开始留意（pay attention to）风的声音、天空的颜色，以及人们窗户里的灯光。  
1. Sometimes I saw children playing outside or neighbors talking quietly in front of their houses.  
    > 有时候，我会看到孩子们在外面玩耍，或者邻居们在房子前轻声聊天。  
1. These small moments made the neighborhood feel more alive and familiar.  
    > 这些小瞬间（moments）让这个社区显得更加有生气（alive）和熟悉（familiar）。  

⸻

1. Walking without my phone also changed the way I think.  
    > 不带手机散步也改变了我的思考方式。  
1. Normally, my mind is filled with information all day long.  
    > 通常，我的大脑整天都被各种信息（information）填满。  
1. I read articles, answer messages, and spend hours looking at screens.  
    > 我阅读文章、回复消息，还会花好几个小时盯着屏幕（screens）。  
1. During these walks, though, my thoughts become slower and clearer.  
    > 然而，在散步的时候，我的思绪（thoughts）会变得更慢，也更清晰（clear）。  
1. I often remember ideas that I forgot during the busy part of the day.  
    > 我经常会想起那些在白天忙碌（busy）时忘记的想法（ideas）。  

⸻

1. Now, I think that being alone for a short time every day is important.  
    > 现在，我认为每天短暂独处（being alone）一会儿是很重要的。  
1. It gives the mind a chance to rest and observe the world more carefully.  
    > 这能让大脑有机会休息（rest），并更加认真地观察（observe）这个世界。
