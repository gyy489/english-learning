# Carrying a Small Notebook

- 时间线索：About a year ago
- 来源：主题2 / 第 3 篇

1. About a year ago, I started carrying a small notebook with me almost everywhere.  
    > 大约一年前，我开始几乎走到哪里都随身带着一个小笔记本（notebook）。  
1. At first, I only wanted a place to write down practical information, such as shopping lists, meeting times, or quick reminders.  
    > 一开始，我只是想找个地方记录一些实用（practical）的信息，比如购物清单、见面时间或者简短的提醒（reminders）。  
1. However, after using the notebook for several weeks, I realized that it was slowly becoming something more personal and creative.  
    > 然而，在使用这个笔记本几周之后，我意识到（realized）它正在慢慢变成一种更加私人化（personal）和具有创造性（creative）的东西。  

⸻

1. During the day, I began writing short observations about people, conversations, and places that I noticed around me.  
    > 白天时，我开始记录（write down）一些简短的观察（observations），内容关于我周围注意到的人、对话（conversations）和地点（places）。  
1. Sometimes I described interesting shadows on buildings, unusual sentences that I heard on the street, or ideas that   appeared while I was traveling on a bus.  
    > 有时候，我会描述建筑物上的有趣影子（shadows）、在街上听到的不寻常句子，或者在公交车上时突然出现的想法（ideas）。  
1. Many of these notes seemed unimportant at the time, but later they often became useful for drawings, stories, or new projects.  
    > 这些笔记（notes）在当时看起来可能并不重要，但后来它们却常常会对绘画（drawings）、故事（stories）或者新的项目（projects）产生帮助。  

⸻

1. Before starting this habit, I believed that creativity depended mostly on inspiration.  
    > 在养成这个习惯之前，我认为创造力（creativity）主要依赖于灵感（inspiration）。  
1. I thought good ideas appeared suddenly and unpredictably.  
    > 我以为好的想法总是突然（suddenly）且不可预测地（unpredictably）出现。  
1. Now, I see creativity differently.  
    > 现在，我对创造力有了不同的看法。  
1. I think the mind produces more interesting ideas when it regularly collects small details from daily life.  
    > 我认为，当大脑持续（regularly）收集日常生活中的细节（details）时，它会产生（produce）更多有趣的想法。  
1. In other words, creative thinking is not only about imagination; it is also about attention and memory.  
    > 换句话说（in other words），创造性思维（creative thinking）不仅仅与想象力（imagination）有关，它也与注意力（attention）和记忆（memory）有关。  

⸻

1. The notebook has also changed the way I experience ordinary moments.  
    > 这个笔记本也改变了我体验（experience）普通时刻（ordinary moments）的方式。  
1. Instead of ignoring small details, I now pay closer attention to my surroundings because I know that even simple experiences can become meaningful later.  
    > 我不再忽视（ignore）细小的细节，而是更加认真地关注（pay closer attention to）周围环境（surroundings），因为我知道，即使是简单的经历（experiences），以后也可能变得很有意义（meaningful）。
