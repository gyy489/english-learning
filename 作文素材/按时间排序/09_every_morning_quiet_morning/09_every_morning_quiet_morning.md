# A Quiet Morning Habit

- 时间线索：Every morning / now
- 来源：主题1 / 第 1 篇

1. Every morning, I wake up at 7 o’clock and make a cup of coffee.  
    > 每天早上，我都会在7点起床，然后泡一杯咖啡。  
1. I usually sit near the window and read for thirty minutes before starting my work.  
    > 我通常会坐在窗边，在开始工作之前读三十分钟书。  
1. Sometimes the street is noisy, but I still enjoy the quiet feeling of the morning.  
    > 有时候街上很吵，但我仍然喜欢清晨那种安静（quiet）的感觉。  
1. The air feels fresh, and the light from the window helps me relax.  
    > 空气让人感觉很清新（fresh），窗外照进来的光线让我放松（relax）。  

⸻

1. A few years ago, my mornings were completely different.  
    > 几年前，我的早晨完全不同。  
1. I often stayed in bed and checked my phone for a long time after waking up.  
    > 我经常醒来后还躺在床上，很长时间都在看手机。  
1. I read messages, watched videos, and looked at social media before I even got dressed.  
    > 我甚至还没穿衣服，就开始看消息、刷视频、浏览社交媒体（social media）。  
1. As a result, I usually felt tired and stressed very early in the day.  
    > 结果（as a result）是，我通常一天刚开始不久就感到疲惫（tired）和压力很大（stressed）。  
1. My mind was busy before my work even started.  
    > 甚至在工作开始之前，我的大脑就已经很混乱、很忙碌了。  

⸻

1. One day, I decided to change this habit.  
    > 有一天，我决定改变这个习惯（habit）。  
1. At first, it was difficult because I was used to checking my phone every morning.  
    > 一开始这很困难，因为我已经习惯（be used to）每天早上看手机了。  
1. However, after a few weeks, I started to feel calmer and more focused.  
    > 然而，几周之后，我开始感觉更加平静（calm）和专注（focused）。  
1. Now, I try to keep my mornings simple and quiet.  
    > 现在，我尽量让自己的早晨保持简单和平静。  
1. I believe that small daily habits can slowly change the way a person thinks and lives.  
    > 我相信，小的日常习惯（daily habits）能够慢慢改变一个人的思维方式和生活方式。
